FAQ

Answers to the most common questions about ordering with DobaleWax.

Just one piece. We specialize in one-off bespoke orders. Whether you need a single agbada for a wedding or one special ankara dress — one piece is enough. No minimum, no maximum.

After your order, we'll send you a simple measurement guide with illustrated instructions. You can measure yourself at home with a tape measure. If you're unsure, WhatsApp us a photo of you wearing a well-fitting garment and we'll interpret your size.

Yes! Many customers send us their own sourced fabric. Just mention it in the order form and we'll advise on yardage requirements after reviewing your design. Note: fabric must be a minimum width of 45 inches for reliable cutting.

Production is typically 7–14 business days depending on garment complexity and our current order volume. Complex designs with embroidery, beading, or appliqué work may take up to 21 days. We always communicate timelines upfront before you commit.

Yes, we ship to over 60 countries. We offer DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) shipping to most destinations so you don't face unexpected customs fees at delivery. Standard tracked shipping is also available at checkout.

We can send physical fabric swatches (small cuts) for a nominal fee that is fully credited toward your final order. Contact us via WhatsApp or email to request a swatch pack of your preferred fabric types.

We offer complimentary alteration service for fit issues caused by our measurement interpretation. Send photos and measurements, and we'll advise on the best fix. Minor adjustments (hem, sleeves) are free. Significant fit issues due to customer measurement error may incur a small fee.

We accept PayPal, major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), and bank transfer. Full payment is required before production begins. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

Every garment goes through a 4-stage quality check: fabric inspection, cutting accuracy, seam integrity, and final pressing. We only use licensed Realwax fabric suppliers. If you're not satisfied, we will remake or refund — no questions asked.

Absolutely. We regularly fulfill family sets, wedding parties, and corporate team orders. Submit one order form per person, noting "matching outfit" in the style notes, and we'll coordinate fabric matching and production timing for you.

Still have questions?

We're here to help. Reach out and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
