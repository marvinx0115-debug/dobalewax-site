Our Fabrics

Only authentic, licensed Realwax and premium African prints. Quality you can feel.

Realwax Classic

Best Seller

The gold standard of African wax print. 100% cotton with a high-wax resin finish that gives the fabric its distinctive shine and durability. Vivid colors, sharp geometric borders, and that unmistakable waxy texture. Machine washable, breathable, and built to last generations. Each bolt is certified by licensed Realwax manufacturers.

  • 100% premium cotton
  • High-wax resin finish
  • Licensed Realwax certified
  • Available in 200+ patterns
  • Width: 48 inches per yard

Realwax Premium Holland

Premium

The super-wax tier. Imported directly from West African mills, this fabric features double-sided embossing for unmatched richness and depth. Heavier weight, denser weave, and a more pronounced waxy finish. Reserved for customers who want the absolute finest wax print available. Expect superior drape, vivid color retention, and a garment that ages beautifully.

  • Double-sided embossing
  • Heavier weight (200 gsm)
  • Premium West African import
  • Available in 80+ patterns
  • Width: 48 inches per yard

Ankara Print

Versatile

Modern digital print on 100% cotton. Broader design range with photographic-quality imagery, landscapes, floral motifs, and abstract art. Faster production time since prints are digitally produced. Ideal for customers who want a specific design — from family crests to corporate logos rendered in traditional wax print style.

  • Digital print on cotton
  • Endless design possibilities
  • Custom design upload available
  • Fastest production time
  • Width: 58 inches per yard

Kente Silk-Cotton

Limited

Handwoven by master Ghanaian weavers. Silk-cotton blend with metallic threads woven into traditional Kente patterns. Each piece takes weeks to produce. Reserved for weddings, graduations, cultural ceremonies, and special occasions. When you want the most prestigious African textile in your garment, this is it.

  • Handwoven silk-cotton blend
  • Metallic thread accents
  • Made in Ghana by master weavers
  • Limited availability per pattern
  • Priced per panel (contact for quote)
