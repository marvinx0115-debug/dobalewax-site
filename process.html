The Journey

Our Process

Simple steps, exceptional quality, delivered to your door.

01

Choose Your Style & Fabric

Browse our gallery for inspiration. Select a garment style from our menu (Agbada, Dashiki, Ankara Dress, Gele & Iro, etc.), or describe your own design. Then choose your fabric from our fabric collection. You can also send us a photo of a garment you like, and we'll replicate or adapt the style.

What to prepare: Style reference images, preferred color palette, occasion type (wedding, casual, corporate, ceremonial).

→
02

Submit Your Measurements

Use our easy illustrated measurement guide. We accept measurements in inches or centimeters. The guide walks you through: chest/bust, waist, hips, shoulder width, sleeve length, and garment length. If you're unsure, WhatsApp us a photo of you wearing a well-fitting garment — we'll interpret your size.

Need help? Our team is available on WhatsApp to walk you through measuring yourself correctly in 5 minutes.

→
03

We Cut & Sew

Your fabric is carefully laid out and cut by pattern makers with 10+ years of experience. Each seam is sewn with precision. We perform a quality check at every stage: cutting, sewing, finishing, and pressing. Complex orders (embroidery, beading, appliqués) may take up to 21 days. You'll receive production photos on request.

Turnaround: 7–14 business days for standard orders, 14–21 days for complex or beaded designs.

→
04

Delivered to Your Door

Your garment is carefully folded, wrapped in tissue paper, and packed in a branded DobaleWax box. Shipped with full tracking via DHL, FedEx, or national postal services depending on your destination. We offer DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) to most countries — no surprise customs fees at your door.

Shipping: 7–14 days worldwide. Express options available at checkout.
