Cultural Festival
Dashiki Ensemble Completed

The Brooklyn Pan-African Festival Dashiki

Marcus O. — Brooklyn, New York, USA

Marcus designed a custom dashiki with full embroidery work depicting the Adinkra symbol of his choice — the Gye Nyame — on the chest panel. We sourced the Realwax Premium Holland in deep indigo and charcoal, and our embroidery team spent six days hand-stitching the Gye Nyame with satin thread in cream and gold. The chest panel took longer than the rest of the shirt combined.

FabricRealwax Premium Holland — Indigo & Charcoal
StyleCustom dashiki with Gye Nyame Adinkra chest embroidery
OccasionBrooklyn Pan-African Cultural Festival
Turnaround19 days (6 days embroidery work)

"Every time I wear it, someone asks about the symbol. That's the whole point." — Marcus

What We Make

Styles Available for Custom Order

Agbada

Gele & Iro

Bubu / Kaftan

Kente Ensemble

Kente silk-cotton blouses, skirts, and accessories. Handwoven by master Ghanaian weavers. Reserved for ceremonies and celebrations.
