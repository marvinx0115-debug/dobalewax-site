Who We Are

From Guangzhou to the World. Dobale — Respect, Then Create.

Based in Guangzhou — the world's textile capital — we source authentic Realwax directly from West African mills and deliver bespoke garments to 30+ countries. Our name "Dobale" comes from the Yoruba tradition of respect: we kneel to honor the craft, then rise to create.
