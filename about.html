Our Beginning

From Guangzhou to the World

Dobale Wax was born in Guangzhou — the world's largest textile trading hub. Here, containers arrive daily from West Africa carrying the finest Realwax fabrics: the same bolts worn at weddings in Lagos, ceremonies in Accra, and celebrations across the continent.

Our name comes from the Yoruba word "Dobale" — the traditional gesture of respect, kneeling with hands to the ground. It's how we approach this craft: with reverence for the generations of West African artisans who perfected wax print, and with commitment to bringing their work to customers worldwide.

We don't just sell fabric. We connect you directly to authentic Realwax textiles and master tailors who understand them — delivering bespoke garments from our Guangzhou workshop to doorsteps in 30+ countries. One piece at a time.

"Dobale — we kneel in respect to the craft, then rise to create."

— Our founding principle

Our Mission

Guangzhou Hub, Global Reach

From our base in Guangzhou — the world's textile capital — we source authentic Realwax directly from West African mills and deliver bespoke garments to customers in 30+ countries. Our location gives us access to the finest fabrics and fastest global logistics.

We don't mass-produce. Every single piece is made to order in our Guangzhou workshop. Your measurements, your style, delivered worldwide.

The Bigger Picture

Why We Named It Dobale

In Yoruba culture, "Dobale" is the gesture of highest respect — kneeling down, touching the ground with both hands. It's performed before elders, at ceremonies, and when greeting someone of great importance. It's not submission; it's recognition of value.

That's how we approach this craft. We kneel in respect to the West African artisans who perfected wax print over generations. Then we rise to do our part: connecting their work to the world through quality tailoring and reliable global delivery from our Guangzhou hub.

Dobale — respect the craft, then create.
